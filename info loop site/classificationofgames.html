Classifying Video Games

Explorer

Explorers enjoy games with rich worlds and open spaces. They prefer to discover new environments, uncover hidden secrets, and experience the world at their own pace.

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Explorers will appreciate the vast open world and the ability to explore different locations, discover hidden quests, and engage with the environment.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

This game offers a beautiful world full of discoveries, where players can explore freely and uncover secrets at their own pace.

No Manâ€™s Sky

With its procedurally generated planets, this game gives explorers endless worlds to explore, each with unique resources and wildlife.

Achiever

Achievers thrive on completing tasks, mastering challenges, and earning rewards. They enjoy progressing through levels and collecting in-game achievements.

Dark Souls

Achievers will enjoy the challenging gameplay and the sense of accomplishment when they overcome difficult enemies and bosses.

Overwatch

This team-based shooter offers Achievers the chance to level up, complete missions, and receive rewards for improving skills.

Super Mario Odyssey

With its variety of challenges and collectibles, Achievers can work toward completing each level and unlocking every power-up and secret.

Socializer

Socializers enjoy interacting with other players, whether through cooperative gameplay or competitive multiplayer modes. They value community and relationships in games.

Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Socializers will enjoy the opportunity to visit friends' islands, exchange gifts, and create their own social community.

Fortnite

With its multiplayer modes, players can team up with friends and compete against other players, offering the perfect environment for Socializers.

World of Warcraft

As an MMORPG, this game offers Socializers the chance to work together in guilds, take part in large-scale events, and build lasting in-game friendships.

Killer:

Killer players thrive on competition and enjoy the challenge of outsmarting or defeating other players. They enjoy games with a competitive edge and ranking systems.

League of Legends

Killer players will enjoy the intense, competitive gameplay and the chance to outplay opponents, rise through the ranks, and dominate the battlefield.

Call of Duty: Warzone

This fast-paced battle royale offers Killer players the opportunity to engage in high-stakes, competitive combat against other players.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

With its competitive multiplayer, Killer players can test their skills against friends or online opponents and rise to the top in rankings.
