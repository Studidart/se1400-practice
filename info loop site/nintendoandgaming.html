Nintendo and Gaming

Nintendo Milestones Timeline

  1. 1981: "Donkey Kong"

    Nintendo's first major success in the video game industry was "Donkey Kong," released in 1981. It introduced the world to Mario, who became the face of Nintendo.

  2. 1989: Game Boy

    The Game Boy, released in 1989, was Nintendo's first portable gaming console and became a massive success worldwide.

  3. 1985: "Super Mario Bros."

    "Super Mario Bros." became one of the best-selling video games of all time and revolutionized the platforming genre.

  4. 1996: Nintendo 64

    The Nintendo 64, released in 1996, introduced 3D gaming to the masses, bringing iconic titles like "Super Mario 64" to life.

  5. 1998: "The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time"

    "The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time" is often cited as one of the greatest video games of all time, setting new standards for adventure games.

  6. 2001: GameCube

    The GameCube, released in 2001, introduced the first online play for Nintendo consoles, paving the way for future online gaming experiences.

  7. 1996: "Pokémon Red and Blue"

    "Pokémon Red and Blue" (1996) was the start of the global Pokémon craze that turned into one of the most successful franchises in gaming history.

  8. 2006: Wii

    The Wii, released in 2006, revolutionized gaming with its motion-controlled gameplay, opening up gaming to a wider audience.

  9. 2004: Nintendo DS

    The Nintendo DS, released in 2004, featured a dual-screen design and introduced touch controls, making it a unique portable console.

  10. 2017: Nintendo Switch

    The Nintendo Switch, released in 2017, supports both handheld and docked gaming, offering versatility for gamers everywhere.
